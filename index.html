TimeOutAI

A free, responsive, AI app to boost your productivity in real time.

Get Started

Built by Programmers for Programmers

TimeOutAI is a free powered by machine learning app which tracks your efficiency as a programmer in real time and give you a personolised advices about your working regime. It shows when you have a peak efficiency or when you are too tired to continue. Based on your personal schedule smart alghoritms will give you a recommendations how you can drastically boost your productivity and become a superhero-programmer!

AI power at your service

TimeOutAI is an open-source project built by Skoltech students during the Ideas2Impact course. This means you can use it for any purpose - even commercial projects! Download it, customize it, and share with your friends! We will be glad to hear your feedback and definitely will try to make our app even better for you!
